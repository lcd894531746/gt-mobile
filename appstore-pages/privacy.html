GT Win Privacy Policy

GT Win is provided by Wuxi Suzhong Electric Co., Ltd. This policy explains how GT Win may process information when you use features such as login, customer management, quote entry, product parameter lookup, printing, sharing, and data dashboards.

1. Information We May Collect

  • Account information, such as username and authentication-related data.
  • Business information, such as customer name, phone number, contact person, address, quote details, product names, product parameters, formulas, and operational statistics.
  • Local storage data, such as login token, saved username, and certain local configuration data used to improve your experience.
  • Information you submit when creating, editing, or viewing customers, quotes, and related records.

2. How We Use Information

  • To support login, identity verification, and secure access control.
  • To provide customer management, quote management, product lookup, and dashboard functions.
  • To store necessary local session information and app preferences.
  • To troubleshoot issues, respond to support requests, and improve the app experience.

3. Local Storage and Permissions

  • GT Win may store login token, user summary data, saved username, and template-related configuration locally on your device.
  • The current version primarily uses network APIs for login, customer management, quote management, and statistics queries.
  • Based on the current app implementation, the app does not primarily collect precise location data, does not directly rely on contacts access, and does not treat camera or photo collection as a core feature.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Data may be processed by the business backend services required for GT Win features.
  • We may disclose necessary information when required by applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests from authorities.

5. Information Security

  • We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information.
  • No method of transmission or storage is absolutely secure, so please also protect your own account and device.

6. Your Rights

  • You may review, update, or delete customer and quote-related information according to the business process and permissions available in the app.
  • If you have questions about information processing, you may contact us using the details below.
